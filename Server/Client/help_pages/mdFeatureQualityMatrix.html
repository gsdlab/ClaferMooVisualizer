Feature and Quality Matrix

In this window, you can filter, sort or compare the optimal variants from your model.
  • Mouse over a cell to observe the concrete unique clafer names and values.
  • Press to add a require(once) or eliminate(twice) filter.
  • Press to fade out any features that are uniform across the table. Press to restore the initial view.
  • Press to clear any filters you may have active.
  • Click on the column headers to pick a variant for comparison.
  • Type a clafer name into the Search box to search for a clafer by name.
  • Expand or collapse clafer trees using by clicking on arrows close to the clafer names.
