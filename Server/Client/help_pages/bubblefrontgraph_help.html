Bubble Front Graph


In this view, you can visually explore the Pareto front in up to 4 quality dimensions simulataneously.

--Drag & drop quality dimensions from the view Objectives onto the graph to decide how they should be visualized (as Y-axis, X-axis, color, or bubble size).

--Hoover over a bubble to see detailed information.

--Click a bubble to select for comparison in the view Variant Comparer.
